About Anonymize.Education

Student Data Privacy Without Compromise

Built on the conviction that schools deserve enterprise-grade privacy tools without enterprise-grade complexity.

Why We Exist

US law reaches any data held by US companies globally through the CLOUD Act. Schools are increasingly required to use AI tools for education, but student data is at risk when processed by commercial platforms.

Zero-knowledge architecture makes student data technically inaccessible—even to us, even if subpoenaed. This isn't a contractual promise; it's a technical guarantee.

Privacy rights must be practically exercisable by every teacher—not just IT departments with compliance teams. We built Anonymize.Education to make FERPA, COPPA, and GDPR compliance achievable through architecture, not paperwork.

Our Technical Approach

These eight architectural commitments define how we protect student privacy:

1. Zero-Knowledge Authentication

Teachers control their data, not us. We can't see student names even if subpoenaed.

2. Local-First Processing

Desktop app works offline. Perfect for school networks with restricted internet access.

3. User-Held Encryption Keys

24-word recovery phrase. You hold the keys, not us. Your data remains yours.

4. Offline Capability

No internet required for core features. Process IEP documents on air-gapped systems.

5. No Vendor Lock-In

Export your data anytime. Standard formats. Complete data portability.

6. EU Jurisdiction Only

German Hetzner servers. GDPR compliant. Beyond CLOUD Act reach.

7. Air-Gap Compatibility

Sensitive school data stays on-premise. Desktop app works completely offline.

8. Reversible Anonymization

Teachers can de-anonymize when needed. You maintain control over the process.
