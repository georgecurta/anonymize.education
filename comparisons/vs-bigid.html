Different Tools for Different Jobs

Understanding what each platform actually does

🔍 BigID: Data Discovery & Classification

BigID excels at finding sensitive data across hundreds of data sources in large enterprises. It scans databases, file shares, cloud storage, and applications to create a data catalog showing where PII, financial data, and other sensitive information lives.

BigID is designed for: Enterprise privacy teams managing data inventories across global organizations.

🔒 Anonymize.Education: Data Protection & Anonymization

Anonymize.Education focuses on protecting student data through advanced anonymization. It detects PII in 48 languages, offers reversible encryption for legal compliance, and works offline for maximum security.

Anonymize.Education is designed for: Schools, teachers, and administrators who need to protect student data quickly and affordably.

When BigID Makes Sense

BigID is a comprehensive data discovery and classification platform designed for Fortune 500 companies managing privacy programs across hundreds of data sources with dedicated privacy engineering teams.

Choose BigID if you:

  • Are a large enterprise needing data catalog and classification across hundreds of sources
  • Have a privacy program spanning hundreds of data sources and multiple regulations
  • Need integration with governance workflows and data management platforms
  • Have budget for a dedicated privacy engineering team

Choose Anonymize.Education if you:

  • Need to protect student data quickly and affordably
  • Require support for multiple languages in your diverse student population
  • Need offline capability for air-gapped environments
  • Want reversible encryption for compliance and legal requirements
  • Need teachers to use it without IT support or training
