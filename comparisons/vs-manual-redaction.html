📊 The Manual Redaction Problem

Manual redaction has led to numerous data breaches when:

  • Black boxes in PDFs were copy-pasted, revealing hidden text underneath

  • Word documents retained tracked changes showing original sensitive content
  • PDF metadata exposed author names and internal file paths

When Manual Review Makes Sense

Manual review still has its place in specific scenarios:

  • Final quality check - Spot-check automated results for critical documents
  • Context-dependent redaction - When the same text should be redacted in some contexts but not others
  • Legacy documents - Handwritten or poorly scanned documents where OCR struggles
  • Legal proceedings - When human judgment is legally required for privilege review

Best practice: Use Anonymize.Education for the 95% bulk processing, then have staff review the critical 5% that needs human judgment. This gives you both speed and accuracy.
