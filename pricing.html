Understanding Tokens

Tokens are like credits. Each operation uses tokens based on text length and detected entities.

Typical Token Usage

Text Length Tokens Used Example
Short text 1-3 tokens A paragraph, email signature
Medium text 3-5 tokens A letter, short report
Long text 5-15 tokens Multi-page document

What This Means

200 free tokens = approximately 15-18 pages of text per month

Note: Anonymization uses slightly more tokens than analysis alone. AI entity creation requires 50 tokens per request.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are tokens exactly?

Tokens are like credits or coins. Each time you analyze or anonymize text, you use tokens based on the length of the text and the number of entities detected. Think of them like prepaid phone minutes.

Do unused tokens roll over to the next month?

No, monthly tokens reset at the beginning of each billing cycle. However, if you top up with additional tokens, those also expire at your next cycle reset. This keeps pricing simple and predictable.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is the Desktop App really free?

Yes, the Desktop App is completely free for basic use. It works offline and doesn't require an account. Some advanced features (like custom presets) require a paid account.

Are there education discounts available?

We offer volume discounts for school districts and academic institutions. Please contact us to discuss your specific needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards and PayPal. For enterprise accounts, we can arrange invoicing with purchase orders.
