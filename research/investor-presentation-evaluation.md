## Conclusion

The investor presentation concept has an **excellent technical foundation** with the 9-pillar moat, 48 languages + dialects positioning, and verified competitive analysis. However, it is **missing critical investor-facing content**:

1. **No investment ask** - investors don't know what you're seeking
2. **No team slide** - investors invest in people first
3. **No traction metrics** - even early numbers are better than none
4. **No unit economics** - CAC/LTV critical for PLG model
5. **No valuation framework** - needed for negotiation

**Recommendation:** Complete the 6 critical items before investor meetings. The technical content (Pages F01-F17) is strong and requires minimal changes.
