## Executive Summary

Page 16 showcases **Custom Engineering** - two flagship products representing opposite ends of the enterprise spectrum:

1. **anonym.life** - B2B Annual Contracts, Zero Knowledge PII Detection Platform with MPC Threshold Custody
2. **anonym.plus** - Perpetual License Desktop App, 100% Offline with Local Storage Only

Together, they address enterprises requiring either cloud-first zero-knowledge architecture OR air-gapped offline processing - capturing markets with a combined TAM exceeding **$60B annually**.

### Why Custom Engineering?
